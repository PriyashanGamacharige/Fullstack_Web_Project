Courses Offered

ICT(Information and Communication Technology)

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of Information and Communication Technology,exploring how digital tools, systems, and networks are transforming modern life and work.

Course Content:

  1. Introduction to Computers and Operating Systems
  2. Basics of Networking and the Internet
  3. Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  4. Productivity Tools (Word Processing, Spreadsheets, Presentations)
  5. Introduction to Programming and Web Development

Engineering

This foundational engineering course provides insight into basic engineering principles,practical applications, and problem-solving techniques across multiple disciplines.

Course Content:

  1. Introduction to Engineering and Design Thinking
  2. Basic Mechanics and Materials
  3. Electrical Circuits and Systems
  4. Engineering Drawing and CAD Tools
  5. Environmental and Sustainable Engineering

Art

Explore creativity and expression in this introductory art course, which covers the essentials of visual art, techniques, and appreciation of global art movements.

Course Content:

  1. Elements and Principles of Design
  2. Drawing and Painting Techniques
  3. Art History and Famous Art Movements
  4. Digital Art and Graphic Design Basics
  5. Portfolio Creation and Art Critique

Business

Gain foundational knowledge in business concepts, including management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship, to prepare for modern business environments.

Course Content:

  1. Introduction to Business and Management
  2. Principles of Marketing
  3. Basics of Accounting and Finance
  4. Business Communication Skills
  5. Entrepreneurship and Business Planning
