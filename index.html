About Our School

The National Institute of Technology & Engineering (NITE) is committed to creating skilled and responsible graduates. We focus on building both technical knowledge and strong values like honesty, confidence, and professionalism.

At NITE, students learn through practical experiences and are encouraged to think critically and work as a team. We aim to use technology to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact on society.
